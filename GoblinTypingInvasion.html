How to Play

Defend the knight from the oncoming horde by typing the words above their heads! Accuracy and speed are key.

  • Type the word displayed above an enemy EXACTLY to damage or vanquish them. No need to click!
  • Typed letters will highlight in green.
  • Backspace removes the last typed letter. Pressing Enter or Space clears your current input.
  • Typing the start of a word locks onto the closest matching target (enemy or power-up).
  • Enemies with multiple health points will require multiple words (they get a new word after each hit).
  • Defeated enemies may drop Power-Ups! Type the power-up word (or tap it) to activate its effect.
  • Consecutive kills build a score Multiplier Streak! Don't miss or let enemies reach the knight.
  • Watch out for powerful Bosses on certain waves!
  • Press ESC or click the ☰ button during gameplay to pause.
  • Survive as long as you can and set a new High Score!
