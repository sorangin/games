In Development

Strategy Multiplayer Action Humor

Description

Arrows, swords, and kidnapped kings... Dive into a hilariously chaotic strategy game where you control a swordsman and archer to outsmart opponents online or against the computer. Sneak through enemy bases by land, air, or underground to capture their king!
