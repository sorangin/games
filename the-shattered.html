Description

Once a legendary king, now a broken prisoner on the brink of despair. When a mysterious demon offers a chance to bring his deceased wife back from the dead, Cedric finds a new will to live. Journey through shadows in this isometric action RPG, battling hordes of enemies and uncovering dark secrets as you follow the demon's cryptic guidance.
